Origin of Skibidi Toilet

Skibidi Toilet originated from a YouTube series by DaFuq!?Boom! in 2023. It features humanoid toilets battling characters with cameras, speakers, and TVs for heads. The name comes from the song "Dom Dom Yes Yes" by Biser King. The series went viral due to its absurd humor, fast action, and bizarre animation, spreading across YouTube Shorts and TikTok. Over time, it evolved with deeper lore and better CGI, becoming a major internet meme and cultural phenomenon.
